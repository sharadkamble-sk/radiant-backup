Top 12 Factors To Consider For Web Development

14 April 2021
77 61

Creating a website with functionality, appearance, eye-catching images, colors & with attractive UI is not a that much easy job. While creating a website you have to consider the other factors also, you have to take care of SEO, content , code integrity etc. and also while working on it web designers & web developers have to work together to load a website on search engines, it should engage customers & also make conversions from that website. It’s a quite difficult job, no?? But not to worry this article is going to help you out to make your website building job easy. In this article, we are to learn about factors which are helpful for you to develop a website-

1. Selecting a domain & host –

We all know that website domain name says a lot about your business. Your domain name should be memorable, no one can misspell it & it should reflect your brand’s voice. Choosing a domain name is some what crucial because you have to consider the SEO factors, simple spellings & brand identity. After considering these factors your website can have higher chances to locate on the search engine. It means that if your website is easily accessible on search engines, you will definitely get more customers. After selecting a domain name, it’s time to select a hosting website, before selecting a hosting website you have to consider the nature of your website & business. For selecting hosting website, consider following factors

● Can your hosting website handle the load of lots of images, videos & presentations?
● Can your hosting website provide you responsive technical support?
● Can it handle a load of traffic?
● Can they provide backup & security?

Before taking the final decision please check out the reviews of hosting services providers from the third party.

2. Backend services –

If you want to develop a website greatly, you have to take care of a functional program at the backend. Without great support from backend it’s impossible to make your customers happy. For building a strong backend for any website you can think about WordPress, a Content Management System & for e-commerce websites you can think about the magneto. Remember that you can show an attractive UI to the user but the engagement of users will totally depend upon how your backend is working for the user.

3. Clean design –

It is the most important thing to consider while developing a website. It’s very important for you to create a clean & appealing design. If your website design is attractive & it’s easy to navigate then it’s a quality design. A clean design helps customers to focus on the brand & content of the website. Often, customers prefer a website with quality design. Therefore, to gain return customers & to give positive user experience clean design is very helpful.

4. Effective Color Scheme –

While selecting colors of your website you have to think about a target audience, branding, company’s niche & elements of the color theory. If you are thinking about selecting a website color scheme depending upon a logo color then it is a good idea but it should not be more overwhelming. So, before selecting a color scheme for your website, spend sufficient time to select colors which can impress your audience.

5. Branding –

Branding is an important factor for every business, whether it is small business or a large business. The placement & design of your brand’s logo say a lot about your business. A professionally designed logo should be placed on the upper left corner of the website, because it is a place from where users actually start to look at a website.

6. Functionality & usability –

Functionality of a website can be measured by considering the factors like there should not be any broken links, there should be less loading time. For usability you have to consider if your website is easy to use, can users read any blog without hesitation, can they share your blogs easily, can they feel a contact form? If the above factors are working finely on your website, then your website is easy to handle & can gain users satisfaction.

7. Navigation –

Easy navigation is the key to a great website. You can place a navigation in the header & footer of the website. If a user can easily access a website’s other web pages then it helps to increase a user engagement. If you are using a navigation which is confusing for your user & which is not placed at a right place, then there are very less chances to gain return users.

8. Call to action –

Including a call to action buttons on your website is very helpful for users. Which helps your users to contact you easily & its hows that your website is genuine & users can trust on it. In call to action buttons you can add a “Contact us today” or “Subscribe a newsletter”. The above call to action buttons will increase the customer’s engagement & you will also get the loyal customers.

9. Less load time –

It is one of the most important factors of SEO and website development also. While developing a website you have to keep an eye on the speed of your website also. Your website’s loading time has to be upto 5 sec. A website’s loading time is very important because it helps users to get the information they want on the exact time. If they are not getting the information on time from your site, they will get it from your competitors.

10. Active blog –

Nowadays, adding a blog on your website is very important, it shows your website is getting updated periodically & you are alive in the world of web. Working on current topics for blogs helps you to get more customers for your website, if you have a blog on current topics or topics which are related to your business shows that you are very updated in your functional areas. Active blogging also helps you to build a brand, increase communication & gain customer’s trust.

11. SEO friendly code –

While creating a webpage or building a website, it is impossible to keep a clean & SEO friendly code. If your website’s code is SEO friendly, it helps crawlers to crawl your website easily. It also helps you to get ROI. If you are using WordPress for your website, you can take the help of some plugins to get clean & SEO friendly code for your website.

12. Effective security –

In this technical world, more advanced security risks are there. There are many threats present like malware, injection of malicious code, hackers due to this your website should be secured on the front end & back end also. If you have an inclusion of payment gateways in your website or you have an ecommerce website, you have to protect a customer’s data & for this protection you have to add a SSL certificate to your website. The above factors are more important to consider while developing a new website which helps you to gain a lot of returning customers, more traffic & trust from users. While considering the above factors there are some other factors also, which you can’t ignore. These factors are as below –

● Compatibility with multiple browsers – Your website should be compatible with all types of browsers, it should not lose its structure & image quality while using other browsers.

● Responsive site – Your website should be responsive for every device,so users can see the same content & design regardless of the device.

● Social connecting buttons – It is a good practice to add a social sharing button on your website. It helps you divert website traffic to your social media channels also.

● Customer Testimonial –When you add a customer’s testimonials on your website, it helps you to gain trust from users. Customers also think that you are genuine & you want to grow your business.

● Original Content – While writing content for your website, avoid plagiarism. If you copy the content from other websites, crawlers don’t trust you & they will not scan your website, which tends to lose the ranking of your website on search engines.
