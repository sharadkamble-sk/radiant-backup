Top 15 Plugins For Your WordPress Site

30 April 2021
44 18

Every business is getting online, so everyone needs a website as a basic step. There are many platforms by which you can build a website. One of the popular platforms is WordPress. Basically, WordPress is CMS, Content Management System, which provides a proper layout to your website. Using WordPress you add functionality to your website very easily. To carry on with this functionality, you need the help of different plugins. Though using themes you can design a website but still, you need to add functionality & smoothness to your website.

Following is a list of top plugins which are more useful to add to WordPress site –

1. WPForms –

As the name suggests, this plugin is used to build a contact form. It uses a drag & drop method to create a form. With the help of this plugin, you can create a contact form, email subscription form, payment forms & other many types of forms. It also integrates all popular marketing & payment platforms, which helps you to build form within 5 minutes.

2. Monster Insights –

It is one of the best Google Analytics plugins for WordPress sites. It allows you to properly connect your website to Google Analytics, which helps you to keep a track of users who visit your website. It keeps a record of how your website is searched & visited by a user.

3. All In One SEO –

We all know that SEO helps you to get more traffic to your website through search engines. All In One SEO helps you to make your website SEO Friendly. It gives you suggestions for improving your On-Page SEO factors.

4. Constant Contact –

In the world of Digital Marketing, email is the best tool for keeping in contact with your users after they leave your site. It helps you to gain customer’s trust & rotation visits to your site. If you really want to grow your business, then you have to make email marketing. Constant contact will help you out with this. Constant Contact allows you to build email list & beautiful newsletters for your customers.

5. Optin Monster –

It is the most popular conversion rate optimization software in the market. Its functionality allows you to convert abandoned users to subscribers & customers, this ultimately gives you more business & sales. This one of the top plugins is more useful for bloggers, business websites & e-commerce websites.

6. SeedProd –

This plugin is used to build a page for your website with a proper layout. It uses a drag & drop method to add components in your web page. It creates custom pages without writing any code. It is mostly used for creating Landing pages. It has a different template for landing pages, sales pages, thank you pages, 404 error pages, etc.

7. WPRocket –

Do you want to increase your website speed? But don’t have technical knowledge for it? This plugin is for you. It allows you to quickly improve your WordPress website speed. It has all features to improve the WordPress website’s speed including GZip,Use of CDN, Data minifying, file compression etc.

8. Sucuri –

Security is the first priority of online business owners. Sucuri is the WordPress security plugin & web application firewall which provides high standard security to your WordPress website.This plugin monitors & protects your WordPress site from malware attacks, DDoS, XSS attacks, Brute Force Attacks & many more.As these attacks badly affect your website you have to add this Sucuri plugin instantly.

9. Uncanny Automator –

It is the best automation plugin. It allows you to connect your existing WordPress plugins together without writing any code for it. Because of this, you can automate routine admin tasks which leads to saving time & money. In simple terms, you can say that this plugin helps other plugins to talk with each other, so they can work together.

10. Google Workspace(G Suit) –

We know that G Suite has small different applications like Docs, Spreadsheets, Forms, emails, etc. which are created by Google. This powerful set of applications is easy to deploy & costs cheaper. A most important function of this G Suite is it allows you to use professional email addresses with your business domain name right inside Gmail.

11. SEMRush –

It is an All-in-One marketing toolkit for Digital Marketers & business owners. It has powerful tools which help you to improve SEO, paid traffic strategy, social media & content marketing strategy. SEMRush is mostly used for competitor analysis, sousing its plugin you can gather competitive intelligence, which helps you to defeat them in SEO organic ranking.

12. LiveChat –

It is another useful plugin for e-commerce websites & business websites. Which is simple to use & allows you to live chat on your website. It has many customization options, so you can use it on any type of device, which helps you to provide real-time support.

13. Shared Counts –

This plugin allows you to add beautiful social sharing buttons to your WordPress website. It also shows you a social share count which helps you to add social proof to your website. In Spite of other paid plugins, the Shared Counts plugin is free with better code quality & performance.

14. WPML –

By default, WordPress doesn’t allow you to easily create a multilingual website. The WPML allows you to create a multilingual website with WordPress. This plugin is very easy to use, SEO Friendly & helps you easily create content in different languages.

15. MaxCDN –

Website speed is the most crucial factor, if your user is far from your hosting location then your website takes so much time to load. CDN helps you to control this issue. MaxCDN helps you to spread your content geographically on different servers. Which tends to decrement in website load time for any location.

The above are the Top 15 plugins you have to add to your website which helps you to improve performance & functionality. There are other plugins that can help you to save your time & energy for building a website. They are listed as follows –

● WooCommerce – is the plugin to build your online store.

● Envira Gallery – This plugin helps you build a beautiful gallery of your images.

● Elementor – It is a visual page builder that gives you the ability to craft original designs for your pages.

● Grammarly – This plugin works as an excellent grammar checking tool, which comes with plagiarism detector, vocabulary enhancement etc.

● Optimole – This plugin is useful for image optimization. After uploading any image on a website, it optimizes it up to that it takes less space on the website.

So, all plugins mentioned above are very helpful for your WordPress website. You can use them or you can find options for them.
