Different Ways of COVID-19 Prevention

19 June 2021
34 20

There is a proverb that “Prevention is always better than Cure”. It is becoming very true about COVID-19. We were assuming that there will not be any sign of COVID-19 or there will be fewer cases of COVID-19 in the Year 2021, but today’s situation does not seem to be like it. After taking some rest COVID-19 is back again with its new mutant. Which is more dangerous than the previous one. The spread & number of deaths are increasing day by day.

Though there is a vaccine available, developing some habits of hygiene or cleanliness for COVID-19 prevention is more important. The vaccine takes some time to create antibodies in the human body & there is a conclusion that it is only 70% effective to prevent COVID-19. As we have already read or we already know that people who have less immunity power or who are already fighting with some big diseases like Diabetes, Cancer, BP, Heart problems are easily attacked by the COVID-19 virus. But, Hopes are still alive. If you follow some instructions of hygiene or cleanliness for COVID-19 prevention, you can save yourself.

There are many sources available that can guide you in different ways of COVID-19 prevention, but while applying or using anyway for it you have to check that what you are following is trustworthy or not? You can take the help of the official website of WHO, to cross-check it.

We are providing you following guidelines for COVID-19 prevention –

Avoid Close Contact –

We already know about it. The mutant of the COVID-19 virus is spreading at a high speed. So, social distance is very important. In today’s situation, it is very much needed to avoid social gatherings.

Keep Your Hands Clean –

Hygiene or cleanliness is a key for COVID-19 prevention. If we keep our hands clean, there are very few chances of an attack of the COVID-19 virus. WHO already has given the instructions of how to clean your hands? Which type of sanitizer is more effective? We need to follow those guidelines to keep our hands clean.

Wear a Mask –

If you’re going out for any type of work wearing a mask is very important & after wearing washing that masks are also very important. A COVID-19 virus enters your body through your nose & mouth, that is the main reason you should not avoid wearing a mask in public.

Use disinfectants –

You have to increase s use of disinfectants to cleans regularly touching surfaces like your laptops, your mobiles, tables, doorknobs etc.

Avoid non-essential travels –

Go outside if it is very important only. Prefer work from home culture for your work.

These are the measure precautions you need to take for COVID-19 prevention. If you follow them you will have fewer chances of attack of COVID-19. These precautions also try to boost your immunity because it plays an important role in COVID-19 prevention.

Stay Home, Stay safe & Stay Healthy!
