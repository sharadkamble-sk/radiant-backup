Top 5 Web Hosting Service Providers

18 March 2021
80 50

In today’s era, everyone is trying so hard to take their business online. They not only want to take the business online but want to keep competition in the business alive. Every business wants to build a website as a first step to enter the online world of business.

While building a website, the first step everyone faces is buying a domain & hosting platform for their website.

While purchasing hosting services for your website, you need to keep the following things in mind –

● Uptime Reliability –

If your hosting is down, no one is able to see & work with your website & you always want to run your website 24/7. You need to choose a web hosting service provider which offers you a network uptime guarantee. If your website gets down then you have to make sure that your web hosting service provider should offer compensation for the same.

● Speed –

A most crucial factor of a website for SEO Rankings. If your website speed is low, your customers exit from your website within a second which is very harmful to your website. That is the reason you need to select a web hosting service provider which can guarantee you optimal website loading speed.

In most cases, if you pay more money to web hosting service providers, then you will get a higher speed.

● Customer Support –

A most needful factor to select a web hosting service provider, because you always need customer support if you make any changes in your website & it may tend to down your website. If you have good customer support you can work anytime on your website.

The above factors are the main factors to choose your web hosting service provider. There are other factors also available which you can consider like pricing & emailing.

In this blog, you will find the best web hosting service providers. Let’s take a look –

1. Hostinger –

It offers the most affordable hosting services in the market. Its pricing starts from $1.39/month. To get this lowest price deal, you need to sign a 4-year contract with Hostinger. You will not get so much storage, but your website can handle 10,000 visits per month without any issue.

In Hostinger as pricing increases, you will get more hosting resources & capabilities. In the business shared hosting plan which is only $3.99/month, you can make 100 websites with 200GB of storage data & unlimited bandwidth. It means that you will get the highest loading speed & data backup for your website at a very less cost.

If you mess your website during any changes you will get a backup of your website anytime.

Another thing that needs to consider is that Hostinger provides you 24X7X365 days support which is very much helpful in your need. It also guarantees your 99.99% uptime with a 30 days money-back policy.

If you are looking for a shared hosting service provider with less cost then you definitely have to choose a Hostinger.

2. DreamHost –

It is one of the oldest web hosting service providers available in the market which was founded in 1996. By its foundation year, you may find it outdated but it’s not the truth. They have great adaptability to new trends in the market that is the reason they are the most used web hosting service provider in the market.

They provide great loading speed, friendly & supportive customer service & security. If you want to change your website you can change it within hours as their adaptability features are great & if you want to make major changes then their technical support team is always available for you.

They know that things can be changed as time passes, therefore they are always ready to help you to make changes to your website.

This is the reason there are more than 1.5 million websites that have chosen DreamHost as their web hosting service provider.

3. Bluehost –

If you want to make your website within a week then you definitely need to choose WordPress, because 35% of the total websites available on the internet uses WordPress as the fastest web development platform with great features & pricing.

If you are looking for the best web hosting service provider for WordPress websites, then you have to go with a Bluehost only.

Pricing of Bluehost starts from $3.95/month & the highest package is for just $5.95/month in which you can access all the useful features.

Your website gets a good loading speed until you load your website with lots of plugins. In the future, if you install so many plugins to your website, which may make your website slow, then you can rely on Bluehost. They can provide you with technical support through active telephone service & live chat.

4. Nexcess –

If you are looking for the best web hosting service provider for your online store then Nexcess is for you only. They offer you a wide range of hosting options with specialty solutions for Magento, WordPress, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Drupal, etc.

In addition, you will get one-click auto-scaling to handle unexpected traffic spikes, 24X7 technical support & an SSL certificate which makes your website trustworthy, so customers can feel safe while browsing your website.

It means that you will get everything to start, manage & scale your online store & this is the reason there are 5,00,000+ websites that use Nexcess as a web hosting service provider.

Though you will not find it as cheap as other web hosting service providers you will have more features, better support & improved performance with the Nexcess.

If you need advanced security & quick growth for your e-commerce website then Nexcess is the best option for you.

5. HostGator –

It is the most useful web hosting service provider for blogging. It is the most affordable web hosting service provider for the beginning of the online posting.

Its basic package starts from $2.75/month which is useful for startups that want to get their website up & running. If you want to access more features then you can choose a higher-cost plan for your website.

Just like Bluehost, they have an active telephone service that you use anytime. They have a huge database of questions & answers with professional opinions & solutions to the common problem. You will find an easy solution to your problem with a demonstration & step-by-step guide.

Conclusion –

You are going to have a long-term relationship with your web hosting service provider. So, choose your web hosting service provider wisely.

quick summary of the blog –

Hostinger – Low-cost shared hosting platform for startups.

DreamHost – Best if you need to do quick & responsive changes to your website.

Bluehost – Best for WordPress hosting.

Nexcess – Best for eCommerce hosting.

HostGator – Best for those who want to start blogging.
